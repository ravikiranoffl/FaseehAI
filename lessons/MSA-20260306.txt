**Today's Focus: Demonstrative Pronouns (أسماء الإشارة)**

Today, we're going to build on our knowledge of nouns by learning how to *point* to them! We're talking about demonstrative pronouns – words like "this" and "that." For our first step, let's master "this" for masculine and feminine singular nouns!

**Notes & Explanation**

In Arabic, just like in English, we use words to point things out. These are called demonstrative pronouns. Today, we'll focus on the two most common ones for singular items:
1.  **<AUDIO: هَذَا>** (haadhā): This is used for "this" when referring to a **masculine singular** noun.
2.  **<AUDIO: هَذِهِ>** (haadhihi): This is used for "this" when referring to a **feminine singular** noun.

Remember how most feminine nouns end with a "تَاء مَرْبُوطَة" (tā’ marbūṭah, ة)? That's your big clue! If the noun is masculine (no ة at the end, usually), use <AUDIO: هَذَا>. If it's feminine (ends in ة, usually), use <AUDIO: هَذِهِ>. Super simple, right?

When you use a demonstrative pronoun like <AUDIO: هَذَا> or <AUDIO: هَذِهِ> followed by a noun, there are two main ways to use them:

*   **"This *is* a [noun]"**: In this case, the demonstrative pronoun starts the sentence, and the noun that follows is *indefinite* (no "ال"). For example, "This is a book."
*   **"This [the] [noun]"**: Here, the demonstrative pronoun is followed by a *definite* noun (with "ال"). For example, "This book." This usage often implies a larger sentence or phrase, but for now, just know how to combine them!

**Examples in Context**
Arabic: <AUDIO: هَذَا كِتَابٌ.>
Transliteration: haadhā kitābun.
Meaning: This is a book. (Here, <AUDIO: كِتَابٌ> is indefinite.)

Arabic: <AUDIO: هَذِهِ سَيَّارَةٌ.>
Transliteration: haadhihi sayyāratun.
Meaning: This is a car. (Here, <AUDIO: سَيَّارَةٌ> is indefinite and feminine.)

Arabic: <AUDIO: هَذَا الكِتَابُ.>
Transliteration: haadhā al-kitābu.
Meaning: This book. (Here, <AUDIO: الكِتَابُ> is definite.)

Arabic: <AUDIO: هَذِهِ السَّيَّارَةُ.>
Transliteration: haadhihi as-sayyāratu.
Meaning: This car. (Here, <AUDIO: السَّيَّارَةُ> is definite and feminine.)
